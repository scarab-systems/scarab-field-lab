# Code of Conduct

Scarab Field Lab is maintainer-facing evidence infrastructure. Keep discussion
technical, respectful, and grounded in public evidence.

Expected behavior:

- Be precise about what evidence shows and what remains uncertain.
- Respect upstream maintainers as owners of their projects.
- Avoid hostile, mocking, or defensive language.
- Keep private information out of public issues, pull requests, and case files.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Publishing secrets or private correspondence.
- Harassing maintainers, contributors, or reporters.
- Misrepresenting AI-assisted work as unattended diagnostic truth.
- Using field-test records to pressure maintainers.

Problems can be reported through the repository security or maintainer contact
channels listed in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
