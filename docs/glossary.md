# Glossary

## Scarab Diagnostic Suite

The mechanical diagnostic layer that checks repository evidence and records
specific findings.

## Field Test

A public record of SDS behavior against a real external repository or public
issue.

## Diagnostic Boundary

The point where evidence shows what expected behavior and observed behavior no
longer match.

## Repair Boundary

The narrow patch scope that may follow from documented diagnostic evidence.

## Human-Owned Submission

A public comment, patch, or PR reviewed and owned by a person, even if assisted
tools helped prepare it.

## Maintainer Authority

The upstream project maintainers' final authority to accept, reject, redirect,
or close a proposed change.
