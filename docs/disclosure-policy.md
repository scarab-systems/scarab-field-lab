# Disclosure Policy

Field Lab records should be clear without being noisy.

Disclose:

- whether the case produced an upstream PR, draft PR, or public comment;
- whether the public patch was reviewed and owned by a person;
- what validation was run;
- what the case does not claim.

Do not disclose:

- non-public SDS materials;
- private correspondence;
- non-public records or generated local files.

When a case involves security-sensitive behavior, use private reporting first
and publish only after the responsible disclosure path allows it.
