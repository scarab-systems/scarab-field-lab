# Disclosure Policy

Field Lab records publish only public, reviewable information about a case.

Records may include:

- whether the case produced an upstream PR, draft PR, or public comment;
- whether the public patch was reviewed and owned by a person;
- what validation was run;
- what the case does not claim.

Records do not include:

- SDS source code, internal diagnostic rules, product internals, or
  implementation details;
- private run artifacts, private correspondence, or other non-public records;
- generated local files.

Security-sensitive concerns are handled through private reporting before any
public Field Lab record is published.

## Field Report Closing Block

Public Field Lab record: https://github.com/scarab-systems/scarab-field-lab

Scarab Diagnostic Suite is proprietary and not open source. Field Lab publishes
public case records, issue links, validation summaries, and claim boundaries
only. If you know of a public open-source issue that looks like a boundary
failure or cross-layer drift, you can suggest it through the Field Lab.
